Technical details

Virtual Try-On (IDM-VTON)
Segments the garment from its background, understands shape/texture/draping, maps it onto the model matching pose and proportions, re-renders with realistic lighting and shadows.
Video Generation (SVD)
Takes the try-on result and generates natural movement — the model walking, fabric flowing, camera angles shifting. Outputs a 4-second cinematic clip at 9:16 vertical ratio.
Model options
4 diverse stock model photos included. Swap in your own model photos for brand-consistent results. Supports male and female models.
Cost per reel
~$0.10 for try-on + ~$0.15 for video generation = $0.25/reel on Replicate. Free trial covers your first ~50-100 reels.
